Family

Tsunami is the daughter of Queen Coral, and the oldest of her sisters.
She has an estimated 46 biological siblings, 35 of which are confirmed to be alive. Of her currently living siblings, she has 32 brothers and 2 sisters.

Romantic Relations

Tsunami has been stated to have romantic relationships with a dragon known as Riptide in a potential future, though she is currently not in said relations.

'Adoptive' Family

Tsunami was raised to see four other dragons as her siblings: Clay, Glory, Starflight, and Sunny. All four are alive.

Parental Figures

Tsunami's mother, Queen Coral, is not a very good mother. Tsunami's father, King Gill, is dead.
Tsunami's first parental figure, Kestrel, was not a very good guardian figure. She is dead.
Her second parental figure, Webs, was not as bad as Kestrel, but wasn't great either. He almost died.
Her third parental figure, Dune, is dead.
